How VitaSense AI Works
Simple steps to get your diabetes risk assessment in under 60 seconds.
1

Enter Health Values

Input 8 medical parameters including glucose, BMI, blood pressure, and more in the Predict tab.

2

AI Analyzes Data

Our Voting Ensemble model (Random Forest + Gradient Boosting) trained on 5,000 real patient records processes your data.

3

Get Your Result

Receive an instant prediction with risk percentage, visual charts, and personalized health tips.

Track & Manage

Use the Sugar Tracker, Food Checker, and Chatbot to monitor and manage your diabetes risk daily.
